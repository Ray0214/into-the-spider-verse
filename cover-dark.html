Spider-Man: Into The Spider-verse

Miles Morales is a normal Brookyln teenager who gets bit by a radioactive spider. He wakes up the next day with spider powers, and notices the arrival of more spider-people into his universe. Together, they must save the day from Kingpin, all the while Miles learns what it takes to be Spider-Man.
