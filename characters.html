Miles Morales

A brookyln teenager who becomes the new Spiderman. He has alot to learn, and is often torn between what his dad and uncle want for him.

Voice actor: Shameik Moore


Peter B. Parker

A washed-up version of Peter Parker who arrives in Miles' world. Through taking Miles under his wing, he regains his spark and doesn't want to live sad and alone anymore.

Voice actor: Jake Johnson


Gwen Stacy

A new student at Miles' school who is the spider-woman of her dimension. Despite starting on the wrong foot, her and Miles become very close.

Voice actor: Hailee Steinfeld


Spider-Noir

A version of Peter Parker from a black-&-white world set during the Great Depression. Peni parker manages to break through his grizzled personality.

Voice actor: Nicolas Cage


Peni Parker & SP//DR

Hailing from the far future, Peni is a peppy, japanese-american school girl living her double-life as the pilot of her father's robot.

Voice actor: Kimiko Glenn


Peter Porker (Spider-Ham)

Peter Porker was a spider who got bitten by a radioactive pig in a wacky world! He's the cartoony comic-relief of the spider-gang.

Voice actor: John Mulaney
