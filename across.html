Spider-Man: Across The Spider-verse

It's been around a year since the events of Spider-Man: Into the Spiderverse. Now, a whole society of spider-people has formed to keep all timelines safe! The society is lead by Miguel O'hara, who believes that certain events must occur in each timeline, including the death of a loved one. Miles, however, refuses to accept this. Can the spider society stand a chance against The Spot? Can Miles save his dad?
